Core Principles

Every design decision starts from these non-negotiable commitments.

🎯

User Control

You author your own Terms of Interaction. AI systems adapt to you — never the other way around.

🧠

Neurodivergent-Friendly

Built for diverse cognitive needs from the ground up — executive function support, sensory adaptation, and energy management.

🔒

Privacy-First

Local processing by default. Your data stays on your device. No cloud dependency for personal information.

🌐

Open Standard

JSON Schema-based, MIT-licensed, community-driven. No vendor lock-in — works with any AI system.

🤝

Consent-Based

Default-deny architecture. Only explicitly permitted actions are allowed. Opt-out is always visible.

📋

Transparent & Auditable

Every agent decision is logged. Full provenance tracking across handoffs. You can review and override at any time.

OTOI Adoption Agents

Two specialized agents to help you adopt and enforce the TOI-OTOI framework.

TOI Agent

TOI Adoption Agent

Helps individuals and teams define, refine, and apply their personal Terms of Interaction.

  • Elicit goals, constraints, and sensitivities
  • Draft TOI v1 with examples and red-flag scenarios
  • Create pre-flight, mid-flight, and post-flight checks
  • Publish TOI artifacts to your workspace
  • Schedule review cadence and maintain change-log

Guardrails

  • Default-deny: only explicitly permitted actions allowed
  • Plain-language summaries with every formal section
  • Opt-out path always visible
OTOI Agent

OTOI Integration Agent

Operationalizes TOI across multiple agents and tools — enforcement, handoffs, and audit trails.

  • Translate TOI into machine-enforceable policies
  • Define agent-to-agent handoff contracts
  • Validate actions pre-execution
  • Surface deviations with remediation steps
  • Produce weekly compliance summaries

Guardrails

  • Fail-closed on missing consent or ambiguous authority
  • Log every cross-agent handoff with rationale
  • Separation of duties for sensitive operations

Quick Start

Get up and running in three simple steps.

Define Your TOI

Use the Personal TOI Template to describe your communication style, cognitive preferences, and privacy requirements.

Validate & Share

Validate your TOI document against the JSON schema and share it with AI systems or teammates.

Orchestrate Agents

Use the Agent Solidarity Kit to deploy TOI-compliant agents that respect your preferences, coordinate handoffs, and maintain a transparent audit trail.
